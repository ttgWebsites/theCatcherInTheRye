Interesting Details

  • Salinger's initial target audience was adults, however currently this is one of the most often taught books in middle-schools and high-schools, in large part due to the protagonist as he is much more relatable to teenagers.
  • Much like the protagonist, Salinger spent his formative years bouncing around from expensive prep school to expensive prep school.
  • This novel has been one of the most challenged books in American literature, and was banned in many school districts for periods of time. Being such a challenged piece of literature has helped fuel the sales of the novel over the last several decades.
